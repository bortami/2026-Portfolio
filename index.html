About Me

Problem Solver. Artist. Maker.

My journey in tech started in 2003 when I built my first website for fun. What followed was a career that has twisted through state parks, restaurant management, event marketing, eCommerce, and eventually landing exactly where I belong: at the intersection of complex business problems and elegant technical solutions.

At Aptitude 8, I design and implement advanced HubSpot-based architectures for mid-market and enterprise clients. That means everything from CRM data modeling and custom UI extensions to integrating HubSpot with Salesforce, Workday, Dynamics 365, and whatever else the project demands.

I have a background in accounting and business management that gives me a data-driven lens most engineers don't have. I don't just build what you ask for. I ask why you need it, what it needs to scale to, and what happens when it breaks at 2 AM.

Outside of client work, I time road races, photograph athletes, paint, and am always working on something new. That's just how I'm built.
